About Us

NITYAY YOGA SOCIETY

Nityay Yoga Society is a premier yoga establishment nestled in the peaceful Shyam Enclave of Panchyawala, Jaipur. Founded on the principles of authentic yogic tradition, we offer a serene and nurturing environment where students of all levels can explore the transformative power of yoga. Under the passionate guidance of our lead instructor Anju Ma'am, every class is designed to address individual needs — whether you seek weight loss, stress relief, flexibility, or overall well-being.

With a perfect 5-star rating backed by over 113 glowing reviews, our society has become a trusted name in Jaipur's wellness community. Our well-structured sessions blend physical postures, breathwork, and mindful awareness to help you achieve visible results and lasting inner peace. Open six days a week from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM, we make it easy for you to integrate the gift of yoga into your daily life. Join us and experience the Nityay difference — where every session brings you closer to a healthier, happier you.
